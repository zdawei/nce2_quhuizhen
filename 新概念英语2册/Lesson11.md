# deserve
- v. 应该得到，值得
## 结构
- deserve + n./pron.
- deserve + to do…

# salary
- salary n. 工资 多指发给办公室人员的工资，一般按月发放
- wages 工资，多指发给体力劳动者的工资，一般按天或星期来发放
- pay 工资 以上两种皆可叫作 pay
- income 收入 不仅包括工资也包括其它途径的收入

# work
- work for/at/in + 公司
- work as + 职业
- work on + 事情

# pay 
## pay (sb.) back (sth.)
1. 还钱给某人
- Have you paid (me) back the money you 
owe me yet? 
- I'll pay you back next week.
2. 报复某人
- I'll pay him back for making me look like a fool in front of everyone

## pay for sth.
1. 为某物付账
- Let me pay for the coffee.
2. 为某事吃苦头或受惩罚
- I'll make him pay for his rudeness!
