# 消息
> C-可数，U-不可数
message [C] 
infomation [U]
news [U]

# away from
- A is … (away) from B A地离B地有多远
- Tianjin is about 200 kilometers (away)
- from Beijing. A is far (away) from B A地离B地很远
- My hometown is far (away) from Beijing.

# in

- “in + 一段时间(’s time)”表示在一段时间之后。

- I’ll be back in two days’ time.
- I’ll be back in two days.

- 若表示“用了多长时间”只用“in + 一段时间”，之后不能出现所有格。

- The bird covered the distance in three minutes.

# 很多
- a great many (of) … 很多，只修饰可数名词
- a great number of … 只修饰可数名词
- a lot of / lots of … 修饰可数名词不可数名词均可
- a great deal of … 只修饰不可数名词

# way
- in this way = by this means 以这种方式
- by the way 顺便问一下，对了
- on the/one’s way to… 在（某人）去……的路上
- in the/one’s way 挡（某人的）路
- in a way 从某种程度上讲
