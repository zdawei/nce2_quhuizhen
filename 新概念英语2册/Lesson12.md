- go to … by water/sea/land/air
- go to … on foot

# proud
- proud adj. 骄傲的
## 如下三个搭配可以互相替换，意思一样。
- be proud(形容词) of … : He is proud of his work.
- take pride(名词) in … : He takes pride in his work.
- pride(动词) oneself on … : He prides himself on his work.

# 一般将来时

## 作用
表示将要发生的动作或情况

## 结构
- 肯定句 S. + will/shall + do
- 否定句 S. + will/shall + not + do 或者 S. + won’t/shan’t + do
- 疑问句 will/shall + S. + do
- shall 只用于第一人称 (I; we), 当代英语多用will 代替。

# small & little
- 都表示小
- small 单纯的尺寸小，little 还包含得有一些可爱的感情色彩（a lovely little girl）。

# across & cross
- 动词 + across(介词) + 宾语 : (跨越。。。)
- cross(动词) + 宾语 + 方式状语 :（跨越。。。）
- sail across the Atlantic = cross the Atlantic by boat

# 短暂性动词
- He will leave for two months. X (leave是短暂性动词，短暂性动词不能一段的时间状语连用)
- He will be away for two months.
- He will go to America for one year. X （同上）
- He will be in America for one year.
- He has joined the army for two years. X （同上）
- He has been in the army for two years. 
- He has been a soldier for two years
